01 // IDENTITY

The Dual Core

Bridging deterministic systems with the unpredictable beauty of life.

After graduating with an honors degree in Computer Science & Engineering, I spent years developing high-performance image processing software and web architectures. However, code alone couldn't encapsulate everything I visualised.

I picked up a camera, realizing that a digital sensor is just a matrix waiting to be populated. The lens became my custom input function, and light became the algorithm. Today, my photography informs my computer vision research, and my CS engineering skills let me build innovative, real-time photographic experiences.
