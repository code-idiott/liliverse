01 // THE STORYteller

Focusing on the Soul of the Mediterranean

Lilia is a passionate visual artist and professional photographer from Tunisia whose work celebrates deep human emotions, breathtaking desert horizons, rich heritage cultures, and raw authentic moments.

Through her specialized lens, ordinary coastal settings, historical ruins, and local market encounters become timeless treasures. She travels extensively across Tunisia—from the majestic Sahara dunes of Douz to the vibrant historical pathways of Tunis Medina—weaving light and heritage together.

03 // DEDICATED Tunisia TRAVEL

Discover the Jewels of Tunisia

Navigate through Lilia's curated travel destinations. Every frame carries the warm breath of Mediterranean history and vast golden landscapes.

DESTINATION I

Sidi Bou Said

Hanging beautifully above the azure waves of the Mediterranean, Sidi Bou Said is an artist's sanctuary, legendary for its blinding white walls and vibrant turquoise doors.

DESTINATION II

Grand Erg Oriental Sahara

The endless dynamic waves of the Sahara Desert near Douz provide golden, wind-carved geometric patterns that capture shadows with supreme graphic perfection.

DESTINATION III

Ancient Tunis Medina

A labyrinth of historical colors, bustling local spice souks, traditional mosaic pillars, and vintage Tunisian architecture dating back over 10 centuries.

DESTINATION IV

El Jem Amphitheatre

One of the largest and most beautifully preserved Roman colosseums in existence, standing tall amidst the warm golden Tunisian sunshine.
